# Final Presentation Requirements

## Format
- 5 minutes per team
- 3-minute presentation (recorded)
- 2 minutes live Q&A (with Streamlit app open)

Record your 3-minute presentation in advance. During Q&A, your Streamlit app must be running and ready.

## Deliverables
- Slides (PDF or PPT)
- Recording
- Streamlit app link

All files (PPT, demo link) must be uploaded to Canvas. Recording must be uploaded to the drive (attach the link here).

---

## Required Slide Structure

1. Problem Overview
2. System Architecture (Detailed Design Diagram Required)
3. Key Components Explained
4. Results + Evaluation
5. Insights (What worked / What failed / Lessons learned)

Your design diagram must clearly show:
- Components
- Data flow
- Agent interactions
- Evaluation flow
